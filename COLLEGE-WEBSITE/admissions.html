Admission Process

Our admissions are based on merit and follow the guidelines set by the University of Mumbai. Here's what you need to know:

Eligibility

Completed Higher Secondary Education (10+2) from a recognized board.

Application

Fill out the admission form online or offline at the college office.

Document Submission

Submit required documents and pay the application fee.

Merit List

Await the announcement of the merit list.

Admission Confirmation

If selected, complete the admission process by paying fees and submitting original documents.

Required Documents

  • 10th and 12th standard mark sheets
  • School Leaving Certificate
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport size photographs
  • Aadhar card

Important Information

Important Dates: Please check our website or contact the admissions office for the current year's admission schedule and important dates.

Fee Structure: The fee structure varies depending on the course. Please refer to our prospectus or contact the admissions office for detailed information about fees for your chosen program.

Scholarships

UBS M.P.S.P.S. College offers various scholarships to meritorious students and those from economically weaker sections. Contact our admissions office for more information on available scholarships and eligibility criteria.

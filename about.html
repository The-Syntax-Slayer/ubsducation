About UBS M.P.S.P.S. College

Nurturing excellence since 2003

Our Story

Uttar Bhartiya Sangh M.P.S.P.S. College was established in 2003, under the umbrella of Uttar Bhartiya Sangh Educational Trust. The college is dedicated to providing higher education opportunities, especially for students in the Bandra East region.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a holistic educational environment through both academic and extracurricular activities. We aim to nurture academic excellence, foster creative thinking, and promote moral values.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution of higher education, recognized for academic excellence, innovative teaching methods, and producing graduates who are not only professionally competent but also socially responsible citizens.

Our Affiliation

UBS M.P.S.P.S. College is proud to be affiliated with the University of Mumbai, one of India's premier universities. This affiliation ensures that our students receive high-quality education that meets national standards.
